About Me

I’m Daryl Escandallo, a front-end web developer recently promoted to Team Leader. With a strong focus on creating clean, functional, and user-friendly websites, I’ve grown not only in technical skills but also in leadership. This promotion has given me the opportunity to guide my team, support their growth, and ensure that our projects run smoothly and on time.

As a Team Leader, I handle both development and admin responsibilities—adjusting ETCs, assisting newbies, answering queries, supporting QA implementation, coordinating with developers, and addressing members’ concerns. I take pride in balancing hands-on coding with team management, making sure that collaboration, efficiency, and quality remain at the center of everything we deliver.
